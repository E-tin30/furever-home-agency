Tips and tricks for dog owners

You might be thinking, "How do I take care of a dog?" whether you're a first-time pet owner or are planning to adopt a new animal companion. Luckly for you this page will tell you all you need to know and has useful links to trusted sources for more information. Note that the tips provided are from the sites linked below.

Tips:
  1. Train your dog early in their life is possible
  2. Make time for your dog
  3. Give them lots of attention
  4. Groom your dog
  5. Visit the vet routinely
  6. Get your dog vaccinated
  7. Protect your dog from fleas and ticks
  8. Maintain a routine feeding schedule (don't overfed or underfed)
  9. Go on walks and make sure your dog gets lots of exercise
  10. Provide a safe environment
